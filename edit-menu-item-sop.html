Editing an Existing Menu Item

How to update a product's details, price, category, image or settings in a merchant's Catalog

Overview

When to Edit a Product

Use this process to update any aspect of an existing menu item — name, description, image, category, pricing, status, or add-on groups. The Edit Product form has identical fields to Add Product.

STEP 01
🧭
Navigate to Catalog
Open the merchant panel → Catalog → Product tab
STEP 02
✏️
Open Edit Form
Find the product in the list and click the edit icon
STEP 03
📝
Update Details
Edit name, description, category, image and settings
STEP 04
💰
Update Pricing
Adjust price, cost, compare-at or tax settings
STEP 05
✅
Save & Verify
Save and confirm changes are reflected on the storefront
Step 01

Navigate to the Catalog

Log into the merchant admin panel and open Catalog from the left sidebar.

Fig 1.1 — Catalog Product list: each row shows product name, category badge, price, stock status and action icons

Catalog List — Action Icons
🔗 Link icon
Copy a direct link to the product's storefront page
✏️ Edit icon
Opens the Edit Product form for this item — this is what you click
🗑️ Delete icon
Permanently removes the product — use with caution
Toggle (blue/grey)
Quick enable/disable of the product without opening the edit form
In Stock / Out of Stock
Stock badge — reflects inventory status if Enable Inventory is turned on
Step 02

Open the Edit Product Form

Click the ✏️ edit icon on the product row you want to update. The Edit Product form opens — identical layout to Add Product but pre-populated with the existing values.

Fig 2.1 — Edit Product form: pre-populated with existing product data (shown: Jalebi)

ℹ️
Duplicate button: The Edit form has a Duplicate button (top right, next to Save). Use this to quickly create a copy of the product as a starting point for a similar new item.
Step 03

Update Product Details

All fields are editable. Update only the fields that need changing — leave everything else as-is.

Commonly Updated Fields
Product Name
Click into the field and update the name. Change reflects on the storefront immediately after saving
Description
Edit the description directly, or click ✦ Generate by AI to regenerate from the current product name
Status
Change between Active and Inactive to show or hide the product from the storefront
Image (Add Media)
Click the × on the existing image to remove it, then upload a replacement. Format: PNG · Size: 512 × 512 px
Label / Tag
Add, change or remove product labels and search tags from the right panel
Order Quantity / Inventory
Toggle these on or off as needed. If enabling inventory, you will be prompted to set a stock quantity
Step 04

Update Category & Pricing

Scroll down to the Category and Pricing section to update price, category or tax settings.

Fig 4.1 — Edit Product: Category set to Desserts, Price £8, Cost Price £0, Margin auto-calculated

What You Can Change
Product Category
Remove the current category (click ×) and search for a new one. A product can belong to multiple categories
Price
Update the selling price — Margin and Profit fields recalculate automatically
Compare-at Price
Add or remove a "was" price to show or remove a discount indicator on the storefront
Cost Price
Update your internal cost to keep margin calculations accurate
Charge tax on this product
Check or uncheck based on current tax requirements for this item
⚠️
Price changes are live immediately after saving. If a price increase is planned, coordinate timing with the merchant to avoid customer complaints on in-progress orders.
Step 05

Save & Verify

Click the blue Save button (top right). Changes apply to the storefront immediately.

Post-Save Checks
Catalog list
Return to the Catalog → Product list and confirm the updated name, category badge and price are showing correctly
Storefront check
Click View Merchant (bottom of the left sidebar) to open the live storefront and verify the product displays as expected
💡
Quick status toggle: To temporarily hide a product without editing it, use the toggle switch directly on the Catalog list row — no need to open the Edit form.
