Autozod — Connect Agent Stripe

Step-by-step guide to connecting an agent's Stripe Express account for receiving delivery earnings.

Role Onboarding

Step 1

Initiate the Connection

Before an agent can receive earnings, their Stripe Express account must be connected. This is a one-time setup per agent.

From the Agents List
Enable Payout toggle
Find the agent's row. In the Payout column, flip the purple toggle ON. A Connect button appears.
Click Connect
Click the Connect button. The browser opens the Stripe Express onboarding flow branded with the Yolulo logo.
💡
The agent completes this flow themselves using their own details. You may need to walk them through it on first setup.
Step 2

Sign In to Stripe Express

The agent signs into Stripe Express using their email address.

Sign in to Express — enter the agent's email address and click Continue.
Email entered — click Continue to proceed to account selection or onboarding.
Sign-in Details
Email address
Should match the email on their Autozod account. Click Continue.
Existing Stripe account
If the agent already has a Stripe account linked to that email they may be prompted to select it or create a new one.
Step 3

Business Type & Selection

Select the agent's business type and choose whether to use an existing Stripe account or create a new one.

Business Type — most agents select "Individual / Sole Trader". Click Continue.
Select an existing Stripe account or "Create a new business". Click Continue.
Business Type Guidance
Individual / Sole Trader
Default for most delivery drivers. If the agent hasn't registered a business entity, select this.
Create a new business
Select if no existing Stripe account applies. The green radio button confirms selection.
Step 4

Business & Personal Details

Stripe requires business and personal information for identity verification and compliance.

Business Details — select industry and enter website (or product description). Click Continue.
Personal Details — name, email, date of birth, home address, phone number. All required. Click Continue.
Key Fields
Industry
Select the option best matching delivery services.
Website
Enter a website or click "Don't have a website? Add product description instead".
Date of birth
DD / MM / YYYY format. Required for identity verification.
Home address
Full residential address including country, street, city, and postcode.
Step 5

Bank Account & Review

Add the agent's UK bank account for payout deposits, then review and submit.

Add an account for payouts — currency, country, sort code, and account number.
Review and submit — all sections must be complete (no "Incomplete" labels) before Submit activates.
Bank Details
Currency
GBP - British Pound for UK agents.
Sort code
UK bank sort code in XX-XX-XX format.
Account number
8-digit UK account number — must be confirmed in both fields.
Review & Submit
Incomplete sections
Any section marked Incomplete must be resolved before submitting.
Submit
Click Submit once all sections are complete. Payout column in Autozod updates to Connected ✓.
⚠️
Verification may take time. Until Stripe verifies the account, payouts may be held. If verification fails, Stripe will request additional documents (ID, proof of address).
